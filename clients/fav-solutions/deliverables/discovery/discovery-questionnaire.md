# Discovery Session: FAV Solutions

> These questions help us understand how your agency runs day to day and where automation can free up the most time. No perfect answers needed. Just walk us through how things actually work, including the messy parts.

## About Your Business

1. **Give me the elevator pitch for FAV Solutions. How do you describe what you do to someone who's never heard of Shopify Plus?**

2. **How big is your team right now? Who handles what?** (Designers, developers, project managers, account managers, contractors, etc.)

3. **What does your revenue model look like?** Per-project fees, monthly retainers, hourly billing, or some mix?

4. **Walk me through the tools you use every day.** Project management, communication, design, dev environments, Shopify admin, analytics, CRM, invoicing. Even the ones you hate.

5. **If you could snap your fingers and fix one thing about how your agency operates, what would it be?**

6. **What separates a great month from a rough one? What tips the scale?**

7. **Have you tried to automate or streamline anything before? What happened?**

8. **Fast-forward 90 days after we finish this project. What does success look like for you?**

## Your Processes (SOP Mapping)

9. **Pick your most common client engagement type and walk me through it from first contact to launch.** Every step, every handoff, every "then I have to go do this manually" moment.

10. **Who does each step? Is it always the same person, or does it shift depending on the project?**

11. **What tools are involved at each step?** (Figma, Shopify admin, GitHub, Slack, spreadsheets, email threads, etc.)

12. **How long does each step take? How long should it take if everything went perfectly?**

13. **What goes wrong most often? When was the last time it happened?**

14. **When something breaks or falls through the cracks, how do you find out? How long does it take to fix?**

15. **Are there steps where someone is basically copying data from one place to another?** (Client info into a project brief, store settings from one project to another, reporting data into a spreadsheet, etc.)

16. **Does the process change depending on the client, project size, or brand vertical?** (Fashion vs. beauty vs. luxury, new build vs. migration, etc.)

## E-commerce & Shopify Plus

17. **How many active client stores are you managing or building at any given time?**

18. **Walk me through how you set up a new Shopify Plus store.** From blank canvas to launch day. Where are the bottlenecks?

19. **How do you handle theme development and customization?** Is it templatized at all, or does every project start from scratch?

20. **How do you manage QA and testing across stores?** Who checks what before launch?

21. **How do you handle ongoing CRO (conversion rate optimization) work?** What does the reporting and analysis loop look like?

22. **When a client asks for a change or a new feature, what does that request flow look like?** From "hey can you do X" to deployed.

23. **How do you keep clients updated on project progress?** Status reports, dashboards, Slack channels, weekly calls?

## Agency Operations

24. **How does a new client go from first conversation to signed contract?** Walk me through the sales pipeline.

25. **How do you scope and price projects?** Is there a standard process, or does every project get estimated differently?

26. **How do you handle invoicing and payments?** How long from work-done to payment-received?

27. **Do you generate reports or deliverables for clients?** (Analytics reports, performance summaries, CRO recommendations, etc.) How?

28. **How do you follow up with leads or past clients?** Is there a system, or is it more ad hoc?

29. **What does your internal reporting look like?** (Utilization, revenue per client, pipeline, profitability per project, etc.)

## Wrapping Up

30. **If we automated your biggest bottleneck tomorrow, what changes for you personally?**

31. **Who else needs to be involved in making a decision on this?**

32. **Is there a timeline or event driving urgency?** (New hires, client growth, seasonal peaks, etc.)

33. **Do you have a budget range in mind, or would you rather we scope it first and show you the ROI?**
