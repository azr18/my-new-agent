# Discovery Session: Savings.co.il (The Israeli Savings Center)

> These questions help us understand how your business runs and where automation will have the biggest impact. No perfect answers needed. Walk us through how things actually work, including the messy parts.

## About Your Business

1. **How would you describe what savings.co.il does to someone who's never heard of you?** What makes you different from other financial advisors in Israel?

2. **How many people are on your team, and who handles what?** Advisors, content writers, customer service, marketing, admin, etc.

3. **What's your revenue model?** Commissions from insurance partners, advisory fees, retainers, or some mix?

4. **What tools and software do you use day to day?** CRM, WordPress admin, email marketing, scheduling, the insurance company portals, spreadsheets. Even the ones you wish you could replace.

5. **If you could fix one thing about how the business operates right now, what would it be?**

6. **What does a good month look like vs. a bad month? What makes the difference?**

7. **Have you tried to automate or streamline anything before? What happened?**

8. **Picture 90 days after we finish this project. What does success look like for you?**

## Your Processes

9. **Let's talk about the magazine section of "חיים בפלוס." Walk me through how a lifestyle article goes from idea to published piece.** Who picks the topic? Who writes? Who reviews? Who uploads to WordPress? How long does each step take? What makes a good lifestyle piece vs. one that doesn't land?

10. **How often are you publishing right now, and how often do you want to be publishing?**

11. **Who is involved in the content process? Is it always the same person, or does it rotate?**

12. **What tools do you use for the blog workflow?** Keyword research tools, Google Docs, WordPress editor, image sources, SEO plugins, analytics, etc.

13. **How long does it take from "we need an article on X" to "it's live on the site"? How long should it take?**

14. **What goes wrong most often with content production?** Missed deadlines, inconsistent quality, SEO issues, formatting problems? When was the last time it happened?

15. **Are there steps where someone is copying information between tools?** For example, research into a doc, then reformatting for WordPress, then manually adding internal links or metadata.

16. **Does the process change depending on the type of lifestyle piece?** For example, a financial wellness tip vs. a seasonal lifestyle feature vs. a consumer trends article.

## Financial Advisory & Content

17. **How does a new client go from first finding your website to becoming a paying customer?** Does the blog play a role in that funnel?

18. **Even though these are lifestyle pieces and not technical financial advice, does anyone still review or approve articles before they go live?** Any internal sign-off process?

19. **How do you decide which lifestyle topics to cover?** Reader interest, trending topics, seasonal angles, tie-ins to your financial services, or something else? Who makes the call?

20. **Do you generate any reports or comparison documents for clients?** How does that process work today?

21. **How do you follow up with leads who come through the website?** Forms, WhatsApp, phone calls? Is any of that automated?

22. **What does your internal reporting look like?** Traffic, lead conversion, content performance. Who looks at it and how often?

## Wrapping Up

23. **If we automated the biggest bottleneck in your content workflow tomorrow, what would change for you personally?**

24. **Who else needs to be involved in making a decision on this?**

25. **What's your timeline? Is there a deadline or event driving urgency?** (For example, a push to ramp up content before a specific season.)

26. **Do you have a budget range in mind, or would you rather we scope it first and show you the numbers?**
