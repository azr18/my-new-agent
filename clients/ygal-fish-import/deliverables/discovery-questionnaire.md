# Discovery Session: Seven Seas International

> We've done our homework on Seven Seas, so we're not starting from zero. These questions help us get past the website version of your business and into how things actually work behind the scenes. We want to understand the daily reality, especially the parts that eat up your team's time across all those time zones.

## About Seven Seas

1. **We see Seven Seas supplies 72 countries and moves 20M+ lbs/year. What's the split look like across your three lines (retail/food service, bait & feed, industry/canning)?**
   Which line drives the most revenue? Which one gives you the most operational headaches?

2. **How many people are on your team, and how are they spread out?**
   We know you have global teams across 18 countries. Who handles order management, logistics coordination, compliance, and customer communication? Where are those people based?

3. **You work with partner processing facilities rather than running your own plants. How many facilities are you working with, and where are the main ones?**

4. **What tools and software does your team use day-to-day?**
   ERP system, transportation management, inventory tracking, accounting, email, WhatsApp, spreadsheets, customs software. Everything, even the systems you've outgrown.

5. **If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about how Seven Seas operates, what would it be?**
   Could be logistics, could be admin, could be communication across time zones. Whatever keeps you up at night.

6. **What does a good month look like vs. a bad month? What makes the difference?**
   Thinking about on-time deliveries, order accuracy, customer complaints, cash flow. What swings it?

7. **Have you tried to automate or improve any of these processes before? What happened?**

8. **What would success look like 90 days from now if we nailed this project?**

## Your Processes (SOPs)

9. **Walk me through what happens from the moment a customer in, say, Brazil places an order to when that product ships from a processing facility. Don't skip the annoying parts.**
   We want every handoff, every email, every spreadsheet update.

10. **Is that process the same for a retail customer vs. an industrial/canning buyer vs. a bait order? Where do the workflows diverge?**

11. **Who does each step? Is it always the same person, or does it depend on the region, the product line, or who's awake in that time zone?**

12. **How long does each step take? How long should it take if everything went perfectly?**

13. **What goes wrong most often? When was the last time it happened?**
    Late shipment, wrong specs, customs hold, cold chain issue, invoicing error. Whatever it is.

14. **When something goes wrong, how does the team find out? How long does it take to resolve, and who gets involved?**

15. **Are there steps where someone is basically just copying information from one place to another?**
    Order details into shipping docs, customs forms, invoices, processing instructions. The copy-paste tax.

## International Logistics & Compliance

16. **How do you coordinate shipments across carriers, freight forwarders, and customs brokers? Is there a central system, or does each region handle it differently?**

17. **Walk me through your customs and export documentation process. How much of it is manual?**
    Bills of lading, certificates of origin, health certificates, phytosanitary certs. All of it.

18. **You hold BAP, MSC, BRC, IFS, and Kosher certifications. How do you track which products carry which certifications, and how do you handle the documentation when customers or auditors need proof?**

19. **Cold chain is critical. How do you monitor and document temperature compliance from the processing facility through to final delivery?**

20. **How do you handle multi-currency invoicing and payment tracking across 72 countries? What does your AR situation look like?**

21. **Private label is part of your business. Walk me through how a private label order differs from a standard order. Custom packaging specs, label approvals, quality checks.**
