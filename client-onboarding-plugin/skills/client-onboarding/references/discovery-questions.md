# Discovery Question Bank

Use this question bank when generating the customized discovery questionnaire in Step 1. Select 15-25 questions based on the client's industry, stated pain points, and company size. Adapt the wording to feel conversational, not like a form.

Each question includes a note on what it reveals for the internal client profile.

---

## General Business

1. **What does your company do, and how would you describe it to someone at a dinner party?**
   *Reveals: Core business model, complexity level, how they think about their own value prop*

2. **How many people are on your team, and who touches the processes you want to improve?**
   *Reveals: Team size, number of stakeholders, coordination complexity*

3. **What's your current revenue model? (per-unit, subscription, project-based, etc.)**
   *Reveals: Revenue structure for ROI calculations*

4. **What tools and software do you use day-to-day? (even the ones you hate)**
   *Reveals: Tech stack, integration points, potential API availability*

5. **If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about how your business runs, what would it be?**
   *Reveals: Primary pain point in their own words, emotional weight*

6. **What does a good month look like vs. a bad month? What makes the difference?**
   *Reveals: KPIs they care about, variance drivers, seasonal patterns*

7. **Have you tried to automate or improve any of these processes before? What happened?**
   *Reveals: Past attempts, skepticism level, what didn't work and why*

8. **What would success look like 90 days after we finish this project?**
   *Reveals: Expected outcomes, helps calibrate scope and set realistic expectations*

## SOP Mapping

9. **Walk me through [process name] from the very first trigger to the final step. Don't skip the annoying parts.**
   *Reveals: Full process flow, hidden steps, where frustration lives*

10. **Who does each step? Is it always the same person, or does it depend?**
    *Reveals: Role dependencies, single points of failure, handoff complexity*

11. **What tools do you use at each step? (spreadsheets, email, specific software, pen and paper)**
    *Reveals: Tool fragmentation, copy-paste points, integration opportunities*

12. **How long does each step take? How long should it take?**
    *Reveals: Time waste per step, gap between current and ideal state*

13. **What goes wrong most often? When was the last time it happened?**
    *Reveals: Error frequency, error cost, recency (urgency signal)*

14. **When something goes wrong, how do you find out? How long does it take to fix?**
    *Reveals: Error detection gap, recovery cost, monitoring gaps*

15. **Are there steps where someone is basically just copying data from one place to another?**
    *Reveals: Low-hanging automation fruit, data entry bottlenecks*

16. **Is any part of this process different depending on the customer, order type, or time of year?**
    *Reveals: Conditional logic complexity, edge cases to handle*

## Industry: Logistics / Shipping

17. **How many packages or shipments do you process per day/week?**
    *Reveals: Volume for ROI calculation, scale of automation impact*

18. **How do you currently generate shipping labels? Walk me through the steps.**
    *Reveals: Label workflow, carrier integrations, manual touchpoints*

19. **Which carriers do you use, and how do you decide which one gets each shipment?**
    *Reveals: Carrier selection logic, rate shopping opportunity*

20. **How do you handle tracking updates? Who communicates them to customers?**
    *Reveals: Tracking workflow, customer communication gaps*

21. **What happens when a package is returned or an address is wrong?**
    *Reveals: Exception handling complexity, returns workflow*

22. **How do you validate addresses before shipping?**
    *Reveals: Address validation gap, potential for automated validation*

## Industry: E-commerce

23. **How many orders do you process per day, and what's your peak vs. slow period?**
    *Reveals: Volume, seasonality, infrastructure stress points*

24. **What platform are you on? (Shopify, WooCommerce, custom, etc.)**
    *Reveals: Platform ecosystem, available APIs, migration complexity*

25. **How does order data get from your store to your fulfillment process?**
    *Reveals: Data flow gaps, manual handoffs, integration opportunities*

26. **How do you keep inventory in sync across channels?**
    *Reveals: Multi-channel complexity, oversell risk, sync frequency*

27. **What does your customer communication look like after purchase? (confirmations, updates, follow-ups)**
    *Reveals: Communication automation level, customer experience gaps*

28. **How do you handle returns and refunds? What's the biggest headache there?**
    *Reveals: Returns workflow complexity, refund processing bottlenecks*

## Industry: Professional Services

29. **How does a new client go from first contact to signed engagement?**
    *Reveals: Sales pipeline, intake workflow, proposal generation process*

30. **How do you handle scheduling? (calls, meetings, project milestones)**
    *Reveals: Scheduling tool, calendar management, booking friction*

31. **Walk me through your invoicing process. How long from work-done to payment-received?**
    *Reveals: Billing cycle, payment delays, AR management*

32. **Do you generate reports or documents for clients? How?**
    *Reveals: Document generation workflow, template usage, formatting time*

33. **How do you follow up with leads or past clients?**
    *Reveals: Follow-up cadence, CRM usage, nurture automation opportunity*

34. **What reporting do you do internally? (utilization, revenue, pipeline)**
    *Reveals: Reporting burden, data aggregation pain, dashboard opportunity*

## Industry: Healthcare / Medical

35. **How does a new patient get from first call to first appointment?**
    *Reveals: Patient intake workflow, scheduling complexity, form burden*

36. **How do you handle appointment scheduling and reminders?**
    *Reveals: No-show rate, reminder automation, scheduling tool*

37. **What does your insurance verification process look like?**
    *Reveals: Insurance workflow, verification delays, claim rejection patterns*

38. **How do patient records move between systems? (EHR, billing, scheduling)**
    *Reveals: System integration gaps, data re-entry, compliance touchpoints*

39. **What compliance requirements affect how you handle data or communicate with patients?**
    *Reveals: HIPAA/compliance constraints, what we can and can't automate*

## Industry: Legal / Mass Arbitration

40. **How many active arbitration cases or claimants are you managing right now, and what does your typical case volume look like month to month?**
    *Reveals: Scale of operations, volume for ROI calculations, staffing pressure*

41. **Walk me through how a new claimant gets from first contact to a filed arbitration demand. Where does it break down?**
    *Reveals: Intake workflow, bottlenecks in onboarding, data collection pain points*

42. **What case management tools are you using today? (Clio, Filevine, spreadsheets, custom database, etc.)**
    *Reveals: Tech stack, integration points, data portability, API availability*

43. **Which arbitration institutions do you file with (AAA, JAMS, NAM), and what does the filing process look like for each?**
    *Reveals: Filing workflow complexity, institution-specific requirements, batch processing opportunity*

44. **How do you currently handle deadline tracking? (answer windows, inactivity deadlines, hearing dates)**
    *Reveals: Calendar management maturity, risk of missed deadlines, escalation workflow gaps*

45. **How are you generating demand letters, releases, and other templated documents today?**
    *Reveals: Document generation workflow, template management, batch processing opportunity*

46. **How do claimants get updates on their case status? How often do they reach out asking for updates?**
    *Reveals: Communication burden, portal opportunity, client satisfaction pain points*

47. **What does your settlement workflow look like from offer to signed release to payment distribution?**
    *Reveals: Settlement process complexity, e-signature usage, release tracking burden, distribution calculation*

## Closing Questions

48. **If we automated the biggest bottleneck tomorrow, what would change for you personally?**
    *Reveals: Personal impact, emotional driver, dream outcome framing*

49. **Who else needs to be involved in making a decision on this?**
    *Reveals: Decision-making structure, stakeholders to address*

50. **What's your timeline? Is there a deadline or event driving urgency?**
    *Reveals: Urgency level, external drivers, project timeline constraints*

51. **Do you have a budget range in mind, or would you rather we scope it first and show you the ROI?**
    *Reveals: Budget readiness, price sensitivity, whether to lead with value or numbers*

52. **Is there anything about this questionnaire or the process so far that we could do better?**
    *Reveals: Client experience friction, process improvement signal, builds trust by showing we value their input*
